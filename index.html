About

Hi, I'm Mike. I live in Austin, TX, and I'm a graphic novel collector, a big Elon Musk fan, a firstborn in a family of 6, and a front end web developer. Born in West Africa, I moved to the US in my preteens, Connecticut to be exact, where I lived, made a host of friends and eventually attained my bachelors degree in business.

I transferred to Dallas, TX, towards the end of my time in University, to help manage my families business, and it was during this time that I developed a serious interest in technology entrepreneurship, reading magazines such as Inc., Entrepreneur and Fast Company.

I remember designing several geocities web pages and a blog in high school just for fun, so while not much I have had some familiarity with coding at a young age. Today I maintain a blog on my journey developing my skills as a front end developer and when I'm not coding, blogging or working I spend time with friends and family spread out all over Dallas, Houston, and Austin,TX. Always learning.
