About

Hi. I'm Mike. I live in Austin, TX, and I'm a graphic novel collector, a big Elon Musk fan, a firstborn, and a front end web developer. Born in West Africa, I moved to the US in my preteens, Connecticut to be exact, where I lived, made a host of friends and eventually attained my bachelors degree in business.

After University I began work with my family developing and managing our family Business in Fort Worth, TX; It was during this time that I developed an interest in technology - reading magazines such as Inc., Entrepreneur and Fast Company. Today I maintain a blog on my journey developing my skills as a front end developer and when I'm not coding, blogging or working I spend time with friends and family spread out all over Dallas, Houston, and Austin,TX.
